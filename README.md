**☕ Coffee Sales Analysis & Interactive Dashboard (Excel Project)**

**Tools:** Microsoft Excel (Pivot Tables, Advanced Formulas, Data Modeling, Interactive Dashboards)

Built an end-to-end Excel-based sales analysis solution by integrating multiple datasets (Orders, Customers, Products), performing data cleaning, transformation, and structured analysis to evaluate business performance across time, geography, and product categories.

Transformed raw data into an analysis-ready format by handling inconsistencies, splitting columns, and creating derived fields. Established relationships across datasets to enable multi-dimensional analysis within Excel.

Applied advanced Excel functions to support data modeling and analysis, including:

Lookup & Mapping: XLOOKUP, INDEX-MATCH
Logical Operations: Nested IF statements
Aggregations: SUM, COUNT
Text & Date Functions: For cleaning and extracting monthly/yearly trends

Developed multiple Pivot Tables and Pivot Charts to analyze:

Sales trends across months and years
Revenue contribution by coffee type (Arabica, Robusta, Liberica)
Country-wise sales distribution
Top customers based on total revenue
Purchase behavior based on loyalty card usage

Designed a fully interactive dashboard using slicers and dynamic charts, allowing users to filter by year, country, and product attributes for quick and intuitive analysis.

**📊 Key Insights**

Geographic concentration: United States is the clear revenue leader (~2054.42), while Ireland (~112.23) and the United Kingdom (~44.87) are much smaller contributors.

Top customers matter: A small set of customers drives a large share of revenue; Derick Snow is the top customer (~251.12).

Product mix variability: Sales are spread across Arabica, Excelsa, Liberica, and Robusta, with different months showing spikes for different types — opportunity to prioritize best‑selling SKUs.
